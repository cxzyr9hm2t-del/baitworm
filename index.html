About Baitworm

Baitworm is a live bait worm farming and distribution startup dedicated to providing premium-quality live bait worms to recreational anglers, fishing charter operators, bait shops, and sporting goods retailers.

Headquartered on a rural property with favorable soil and climate conditions, we breed, raise, harvest, and sell a variety of live worms including nightcrawlers, red wigglers, and European nightcrawlers.

Our mission is to become the most trusted and reliable source of premium live bait worms by combining sustainable vermiculture practices with modern packaging and direct-to-consumer delivery logistics.
